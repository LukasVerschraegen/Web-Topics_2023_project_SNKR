Description:

The New Balance 880 v12 has a neutral stride. Thanks to the Fresh Foam cushioning you benefit from a soft and lightweight feel. The mesh upper provides extra comfort and has increased breathability.
In addition, the New Balance 880 v12 has a tight fit without the running shoe feeling trapped feel. The rubber outsole is abrasion-resistant, making the running shoe last longer.

The difference between the V11 and the V12 is that the midsole materials have been "flipped. The material with the highest energy return is now closest to the ground under the forefoot for a dynamic stride. Under the heel and continuing under the forefoot, the composition of the material is such that this provides the optimal combination between soft cushioning and a stable platform.

The same combination is incorporated into the wear sole as stiffer rubber with more abrasion resistance is applied under the heel and medial side of the midfoot. This provides slightly more torsional stiffness and increases the number of miles you can run on the 880 v12. The forefoot features a slightly softer rubber to work together with the midsole to ensure a dynamic stride.
